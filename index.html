About Me

Full Stack Developer / LLMops-Enthusiast !

Hello, my name is Hanan Wandji. I am a 23-year-old Master's student in Computer Science at Hochschule Worms, specializing in Software Development and Data Analysis. Previously, I completed my Bachelor’s degree at the University of Applied Sciences Kaiserslautern, where I graduated with a solid academic foundation and hands-on experience in the tech field. Throughout my career, I’ve cultivated a wealth of experience across diverse roles and projects. As a Working Student in Software Development at Pit-Cup GmbH, I contributed by enhancing technologies and creating automated tests for web applications using Selenium IDE. During my internship at Mercedes-Benz Bank, I played a key role in implementing an interface to streamline data uploads into a GRC-Tool, emphasizing CI/CD pipeline development with GitHub. At Camline GmbH, my responsibilities spanned two impactful roles. As a Working Student, I developed an automated interface to upload Jira issues from CSV files, leveraging Python, FAST-API, and the Jira REST-API, while also setting up pipelines with GitLab and Jenkins. My Bachelor project at the same company focused on designing a Python application for consistency checks in complex operational datasets, which involved requirement analysis, software implementation, and rigorous testing. Currently, as a Working Student at Actico GmbH, I focus on backend development, feature enhancements, and testing, employing technologies like Java, Spring Boot, and Docker. In addition to my professional roles, I actively explore emerging technologies, such as Generative AI and data visualization. I have led projects like creating an interactive medical visualization application using Python and VTK, and a web-based integration platform for newcomers to Germany, combining Angular, Java, and AI-driven chatbots. My technical expertise spans Java, Python (FAST-API), C/C++, Spring Boot, Angular, Jenkins, Docker, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Git, and more. I thrive in environments that challenge me to push my boundaries and innovate. With a passion for continuous learning, I stay updated on industry trends and embrace new challenges as opportunities to grow and contribute meaningfully.
