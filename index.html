About Me

Full Stack Developer / LLMops-Enthusiast !

Hello, my name is Hanan Wandji. I am a 24-year-old Master's student in Computer Science at Hochschule Worms, specializing in Software Development and Data Analysis. My academic journey, complemented by extensive hands-on experience across multiple companies, has shaped me into a versatile and solution-driven software developer. Currently, as a Master’s student at Satware AG, I am developing a local autonomous Multi-Agent platform (SAAP) for enterprise AI agent deployment. The project focuses on designing scalable, privacy-preserving, and resource-efficient architectures, combining Python (FastAPI/Django), Vue.js, PostgreSQL, Redis/RabbitMQ, and Docker to enable autonomous collaboration between specialized AI agents. Alongside my academic research, I work as a Working Student at Actico GmbH, where I contribute to backend development, feature implementation, and testing using Java, Spring Boot, Docker, and CI/CD pipelines. Before that, at Camline GmbH, I held two impactful roles — as a Working Student, I developed an automated Jira integration interface with Python and FastAPI, and during my Bachelor thesis, I designed a Python application for data consistency checks within complex manufacturing systems, focusing on reliability and automation. Earlier, I gained practical experience at Mercedes-Benz Bank AG, where I supported the implementation of a GRC data upload interface and built CI/CD pipelines with GitHub. My journey began at Pit-Cup GmbH, enhancing web technologies and automating testing workflows with Selenium IDE. Beyond professional roles, I enjoy exploring Generative AI, data visualization, and intelligent systems. My projects include interactive medical visualization tools with Python & VTK and a web-based integration platform for newcomers in Germany, built with Angular, Java, and AI-driven chatbots. With strong analytical thinking, a passion for clean and efficient code, and a constant drive for innovation, I thrive in dynamic environments that challenge me to grow — turning complex ideas into impactful software solutions. — Hanan Wandji ☺
